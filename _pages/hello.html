From Data to Stories

Datawheel is a small but mighty crew of programmers and designers who are here to make sense of the world’s vast amount of data. We know how to turn any client’s raw data into an interactive interface that is clear, accessible and beautiful.

Data USA
Data USA is, to date, the largest and most comprehensive representation of U.S. data online.
DataViva
DataViva is a ground-breaking, publicly-accessible web platform representing economic information from all of Brazil.
D3Plus
D3plus acts as an extension to the wildly popular D3 library. With it, beautiful data visualizations are possible in only a few lines of code.
Observatory of Economic Complexity
The Observatory of Economic Complexity visualizes over 50 years of UN Comtrade data in one easy to use, public interface.
