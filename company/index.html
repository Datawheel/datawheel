The people behind the code

Datawheel, LLC was founded in 2013 by César Hidalgo, Alexander Simoes, and Dave Landry. Each bringing their own area of expertise to the table, they are able to approach problems with a wide breadth of experience and know-how.

César Hidalgo

Co-founder

Cesar is the ABC Career Development Professor at the MIT Media Lab and directs the Macro Connections group. He has ten years of experience on the analysis and creation of tools for the representation and understanding of metadata.

Professor Hidalgo has been involved in more than 30 academic publications exploring the possible uses of economic data, health data, mobile phone data, and urban perception data.

Alex Simoes

Co-founder

Alex is a graduate of the MIT Media Lab, where he worked to develop data decision-making tools using visual techniques to explore large datasets.

As part of his Master's Thesis he developed The Observatory of Economic Development, a website used to visualize world trade flows with 50 years worth of data from more than 200 countries and 2000 products.

Dave Landry

Co-founder

Dave graduated from Northeastern University with a dual-degree in Graphic Design and Multimedia Studies, and has a background in print media, interactive design, and programming. His mission is to simplify and beautify the world of data visualization, one pixel at a time.

His other past experience includes working as Producer for a video game development studio and as Art Director for a Boston-based music magazine.
